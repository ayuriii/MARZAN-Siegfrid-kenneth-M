Secure Payment

When you place your order with us, we authorize your card, placing a hold on the funds in your account. Authorization holds are removed from your account within 24-72 hours, depending on your financial institution’s policies. Settlement When we ship your order, we will “settle”s the authorization on your card for the items we are shipping, plus applicable taxes and shipping charges. Sometimes, if the authorization has not cleared your account, it may look like there are two charges on your account. Additional Charges This commonly happens if you request a used book, but only new copies are available. As a result, a separate charge may appear on your statement for the difference between the price of a used book and the new book. On other occasions additional charges are collected as a result of additional shipping charges

About Us

Our platform gives independent bookstores tools to compete online and financial support to help them maintain their presence in local communities.
